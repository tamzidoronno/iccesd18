Welcome

Welcome to the 4th consecutive event of the International Conference on Civil Engineering for Sustainable Development (ICCESD 2018) organized by the Department of Civil Engineering, Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Khulna, Bangladesh. The 1st, 2nd and 3rd events of the ICCESD conference were held in 2012, 2014 and 2016, respectively, and received more than 200 delegates from all around the world in each event. ICCESD aims to provide a platform for exchanging ideas on latest advances in research and innovations, as well as state-of-the-art information in the fields of Civil Engineering among Academicians, Scientists, Researchers and Civil Engineering Practitioners.

The Organizing Committee invites academics, engineers, architects, researchers, and developers to present paper(s) and/or participate in the conference. The success and achievement gained in the previous ICCESD conferences have been remarkable and we are confident that this ICCESD 2018 will bring significant benefits to the participants too.



Conference Venue

The Department of Civil Engineering, Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Khulna 9203, Bangladesh is the venue of the conference.



Conference Themes

The ICCESD 2018 conference focuses on a wide range of issues of Civil Engineering to enhance the knowledge-base, increase awareness, explore new ideas and thinking. The technical sessions will consist of plenary and keynote lectures, in which both the leading experts and young researchers will present state-of-the-art reviews of rapidly-developing and exciting areas, report the latest significant findings and developments in all the major fields of Civil Engineering as follows:

  • Structural and Earthquake Engineering
  • Geotechnical and Geological Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Transportation Planning and Traffic Management
  • Water Resources Engineering
  • Construction Engineering and Management
  • Fire Engineering.

Abstract submission begins on May 01, 2017

Abstract

An abstract, within a maximum of 250 words should be submitted electronically. The abstract should state briefly the purpose of the research, methodologies, the principal results and major conclusions with a maximum of five (05) keywords. All contributing authors should be listed with their affiliations and email addresses and with the indication of the corresponding author with complete postal address.


Full Paper & Executive Summary

Author(s) of accepted abstract need to submit a two-page executive summary and a full paper in the specified templates. The maximum length of full paper is twelve pages. The templates can be found at the "Submission" page.

Official Language
The official language of the conference is English. All submissions, presentations and discussions shall be in English.
For any queries send email to iccesd2018@gmail.com


Best Paper Awards

Best paper awards will be presented to the distinguished author(s) from each major branches of civil engineering.
